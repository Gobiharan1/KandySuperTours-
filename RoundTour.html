Itinerary Details

Day 1: Arrival & The Royal City

Arrival in Kandy / City hotel. Kandy hotel city. Arrival to to your hotel in Colombo. Start at Sri Lanka's holiest temple, the Royal Botanical Gardens. Stop for lunch. Followed by a stopover at Kandy Lake to unwind with the Kandy Lake Kandy. City walk.

Day 2: Botanical Wonders & Golden Temples

Botanical Gardens. Dambulla Cave Temple / Sacred Temple / Dambulla cave temple. Arriving until the Kandy Gardens, Journey north to the famous Cave Temples, a UNESCO site with 2000-year-old murals. Nearby to the cave of Sigiriya for a scenic evening.

Day 3: The Lion's Fortress & Return

Sigiriya / Spice Garden / Kandy / Return. Explore a UNESCO site: Sigiriya Lion Rock Fortress spanning 1200 metres! The Sigiriya Lion Rock Fortress is one of Sri Lanka's greatest monuments! From Sigiriya onwards to Colombo or Colombo for your departure.
