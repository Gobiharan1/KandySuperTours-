About the Family

Welcome to Kandy
Super Tours

Hello and welcome! We are a trusted, family-owned tour company based in the beautiful hill capital of Kandy. Founded by Mr. Prasanna Malalpola, with his son Ravishan, we bring decades of experience and a deep love for our homeland to every journey.

Our mission is simple: to provide international travelers with safe, comfortable, and deeply authentic Sri Lankan experiences. Whether you're exploring ancient ruins or mist-covered tea hills, our professional drivers ensure you feel like a guest, not just a tourist.
