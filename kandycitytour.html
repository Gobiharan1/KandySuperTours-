A Welcome to the Hill Capital

Kandy is more than a city; it is the spiritual soul of Sri Lanka. Nestled amidst the central highlands, this UNESCO World Heritage site was the last capital of the ancient kings' era. Our full-day tour invites you to explore its mist-covered mountains, vibrant markets, and sacred traditions.

Designed for comfort and depth, this experience is ideal for families and couples seeking a refined encounter with Sri Lankan culture. Relax in a private air-conditioned vehicle as our expert guides unveil the secrets of the Kandyan kingdom.
